Reflection

Having discovered so much about my family history, it has influenced me to reflect and think about how my intersectional identities and interests. My passion for academics, arts, and athletics is inherited from various parts of my ancestry. From my aunt, I’ve explored my creativity, sampling drawing and painting, photography, sculpture, and ceramics. From my Por Por and Yin Yin, I’ve probed the diverse cultures of Asia and studied Mandarin. From my Mom and Dad, I embraced my love for innovation and design thinking, business, entrepreneurship. From my Yer Yer, I have discovered my interest in technology, engineering, and computer science. From my brother and cousins, I’ve gained the confidence to be a leader on the field while competing in soccer, tennis, track and field, and basketball.

This is a story of family and discovery!
